Key Features

Manage Quizzes and Examinations

  • Create CBTs or examinations for your students.
  • Schedule tests for a future date.
  • Automatically grade students based on their scores in tests/examinations.

Track Students' Progress

  • Monitor and keep track of students progress.

Upcoming Examinations at a glance

  • View all upcoming examinations and quizzes in the `Upcoming Examinations` tab.

About eCity

eCity is a CBT (Computer Based Test) app developed by Alexander Udochukwu Ikpeama (a student at ALX Holberton School of Software Engineering) as his portfolio project which spanned three (3) weeks (19th May, 2023 to 9th June, 2023).

eCity was developed by Alexander in his hope to lessen the workload on teachers around the world.
